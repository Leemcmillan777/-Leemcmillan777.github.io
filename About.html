About Us

At Legros Landscape and Construction, we are dedicated to creating exceptional outdoor spaces that reflect the natural beauty of New Zealand. With our expertise in native revegetation and residential landscaping, we bring together professionalism, creativity, and sustainability to transform your vision into reality.

Our team consists of experienced landscape designers, horticulturists, and construction specialists who are passionate about delivering high-quality projects that exceed client expectations. We combine our deep understanding of native plant species with innovative design principles to craft landscapes that are visually stunning and ecologically sustainable.

Key Attributes:

  • Passionate about native revegetation and residential landscaping
  • Professionalism and attention to detail in every project
  • Creative designs that reflect the natural beauty of New Zealand
  • Expertise in sustainable practices and ecological balance
  • Commitment to exceeding client expectations
  • Experienced team of landscape designers, horticulturists, and construction specialists
